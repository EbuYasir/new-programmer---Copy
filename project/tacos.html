Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United Stated?

Tacos made their first appearance in the United States in a newspaper in 1905. The publicity that the tacos got was thanks to the Chili Queens of San Antonio. The Chili Queens were a group of women who made traditional mexican dishes at the American festivals. Since Mexico was considered dangerous territory the Americans, the Americans went to the Chili Queens to still taste mexican food and mexican culture1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
